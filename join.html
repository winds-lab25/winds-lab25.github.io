Join the lab

We’re looking for motivated students who like to build systems and then measure whether they hold up.

Who we’re looking for

Builders & measurers

The lab welcomes students at the Master’s and PhD levels, preferably with a Computer Science background. Curiosity, persistence, and a taste for hands-on systems work matter more than a perfect CV.
